Our Services

Services that we provide
Market for farmers

Farmers doesn’t get right price for their crops in market mechanism due to trade transparency issue. OriginConnect is providing several options to Farmers to sell their crop to get expected price. Farmers will not be mercy of few traders and also local mandi. We will enable them to explore options across India. OriginConnect will connect them reliable and trusted customers to deal directly. OriginConnect will handhold them to ensure profitable and transparent trade.

Sourcing for Customers

Customers doesn’t get consistent quality supply of agri produces, creates trust issues which leads to credit cycle challenges at both ends. We not only provide options to sourcing,but authenticate credibility of Farmer as well as produce. We will become your extended arm at the source to ensure onsite supervision, provide photos, videos, so that you are sure that quality of produce is as per your expectations to avoid surprises.

Logistics support

OriginConnect not only connect demand to supply, but also ensures logistics support like arranging transportation, warehousing at source as well as at near to demand as per customer expectations. We will also provide traceability of produce thru GPS linked to platform, so that customer can plan his activities in advance. Our objective is to reduce wastage of produce across value chain to half with use of cold chain (warehousing and transportation). It will add to profitability of Farmer as well as Customer. OriginConnect Team at source will coordinate for all logistics support so that customers can focus on their core activities.

Grading support

OriginConnect believes that trust deficit issues in agri trade can be substantially reduced if we have accurate grading of produce at source. We are introducing optical grading machin, which will not only ensure accurate grading with high speed, but only grading results can be uploaded on platform so that customer is sure on what is he sourcing. Grading also provides good price to Farmer for his crop and also help to reduce wastage in transit.

Customised packaging

We understands the requirement of different customers and provide customized packaging based on requirement. This reduces manual effort at demand end, which support to minimize cost and time.

Finance Services

Farmers as well as Customers has limitations to grow their business mainly due to working capital issue. OriginConnect understands this challenge and providing financial assistance like bill discounting etc by collaborating with financial institutes.

GRV Junction AgriTech Pvt Ltd is founded with the dream of adding value to farmers by enabling fare price to their crops as well as ensuring consistent quality supply to customers. Started in early 2020 by passionate founders Ajit Jangle and Ravish Yadav, who has complementary skills of running large supply chain companies and working with farmers and wholesalers in mandis. Company is not only provide B2B Agri Trading platform, but also ensures effective operational support by value additions like grading, sorting, packaging, cold chain transportation, customized Delivery and financial assistance like bill discounting to enable trade. We are sourcing from Gujarat, Rajastan, Maharastra and Uttarpradesh, where our customer base is mainly in Tamilnadu, Karnataka and Kerala.
